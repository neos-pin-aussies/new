Exam FAQs

How do I get my admit card?

Admit cards appear in your dashboard 7 days before the exam. You’ll also receive an email with a download link.

What if I have a clash?

Submit a clash request within 48 hours of schedule announcement. Alternate slots are subject to availability.

Are calculators allowed?

Basic calculators are allowed for Data Science & Business exams. Programmable devices are not permitted.

When are results declared?

Objective tests: within 5–7 days. Practicals/portfolios: within 10–14 days after evaluation.
